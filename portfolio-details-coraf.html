Job / Project information

  • Category: Monitoring and Evaluation
  • Company / Client: West and Central African Council for Agricultural Research and Development (CORAF)
  • Period: September 2019 - Present
  • URL: www.coraf.org

Description

As the M&E Lead Specialist of the West and Central African Council for Agricultural Research and Development (CORAF), my main responsibility is to develop and implement the Performance and Results Measurement Framework of the institution by providing consistent approach for systematically collecting, analysing, utilizing, and reporting on the performance of CORAF’s programs, projects, and activities.

Key duties include:

  • Develop reliable and robust M&E approaches, procedures, and tools to monitor the performance and impacts of CORAF intervention across West and Central Africa: Currently coordinating a portfolio of 7 projects funded by various donors including USAID, EU, Swiss Cooperation, IDRC, World Bank Group, etc. in various thematic (including agricultural technologies and innovations scaling up, climate smart agriculture, capacity strengthening, women and youth empowerment through agriculture, etc.).
  • Develop and implement appropriate monitoring and evaluation tools, including the design and management of country and partner-level systems, the preparation of indicator reference sheets, performance management plans (PMP), data collection tools, mechanisms for tracking performance indicators, assessment of data quality.
  • Design and coordinate baseline and reference studies to collect information on performance indicators of programmes coordinated by the Institution: 2 baseline studies designed and coordinated in 2021.
  • Support project teams to conduct mid-term and final evaluations: Developed the TOR of the final evaluation of one project.
  • Prepare quarterly, semi-annual, and annual reports, and be involved in the development of periodic newsletters and success stories on the performance of CORAF’s intervention in West and Central Africa: Prepared 4 quarterly reports, 2 semi-annual reports and 1 annual report in 2021 and involved in the preparation of 10+ accountability documents.
  • Design and coordinate M&E capacity building programs for partners: Organized 1 training in M&E for 20+ country actors in West Africa and 1 training in impact assessment for 20+ experts in West and Central Africa.
  • Provide technical and analytical expertise on all matters related to program M&E and organize annual reviews as required.
  • Carry out Data Quality Assessments: 8 Data Quality Assessment mission conducted in six countries in West Africa.
  • Support the formulation of new programs by assisting proposal writing committee in Theory of Change design and M&E related tools: Involved in the formulation of the West Africa Food System Resilience Program (FSRP) funded by WBG.
  • Carry out other M&E and reporting tasks that may be requested by CORAF Management.
