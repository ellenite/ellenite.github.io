Job / Project information

  • Category: Monitoring and Evaluation
  • Company / Client: ECOWAS Commission - Directorate of Agriculture and Rural Development
  • Period: August 2017 - September 2020
  • URL: ecowap.ecowas.int

Description

As M&E assistant in the Directorate of Agriculture & Rural Development (DARD) recruited by Permanent Interstate Committee for Drought Control in the Sahel (CILSS), my key responsibility was to provide high technical assistance and support to ECOWAS Agricultural Policy (ECOWAP) Monitoring & Evaluation Mechanism.

Key duties include:

  • Provide technical assistance and support to the design and implementation of M&E activities, including performance monitoring plan development, data collection tools and methodologies, data entry, analysis, and reporting: Involved in the preparation of the African Agriculture Transformation Scorecard from 2017 – 2020 as member of the continental network of M&E experts in charge of developing the biennial report on the Malabo Declaration.
  • Assist in monitoring, documenting, and communicating the processes, challenges, and achievements of implementation of the organization’s activities in both qualitative and quantitative terms: Elaboration of one regional M&E report capturing key achievements over 2016-2018 implementation period of the ECOWAP.
  • Ensure timely collection (e.g., surveys, interviews), storage and processing of data in electronic applications systems: Development of the web-based M&E System for ECOWAP / CAADP to enhance performance management as well as data collection and management.
  • Support the M&E team with accurate and timely entry and management of data including carrying out analytical work: Coordination of the second CAADP Biennial Review process at ECOWAS Region level to show progress of regional agriculture investment plan implementation across 15 countries.
  • Suggest overall M&E system improvement tools and procedures: Development of a user friendly excel version of the Biennial Review database for ECOWAS countries to allow reporting and analysis.
  • Assist in developing and writing up results and success stories for organizational reports, and any marketing materials for the project.
  • Provide technical and analytical expertise on all matters related to program M&E and organize annual reviews as required.
  • Assist in the dissemination of various program documents such as newsletters, success stories and other related documents to various stakeholders when required.
  • Provide support during assessment activities, meetings and workshops carried out by the organization.
